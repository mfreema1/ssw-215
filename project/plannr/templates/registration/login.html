About the Plannr Project

Hi! I'm actually a project created in one month by two developers at Stevens Institute of Technology! I was made as an introductory effort in Django with no prior knowledge of the framework, so please excuse any mistakes you may find during use. In fact, if you find any bugs, please send them along to Mark at his email.


I'm currently in prototype stages and am being offered as a free service for people to keep track of their lives via the web. We wanted to operate off of a similar model to some popular planning systems in print, so that's essentially what we ported to web through this project. At it's heart, this is a CRUD application, so Django was chosen to facilitate much of the effort of those operations. (This project had a deadline after all!)
