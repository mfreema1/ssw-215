Your Daily Entries:

Here you can view all of the daily entries you've made in your Plannr. Daily entries will generally consists of a few things -- such as an affirmation, which is basically a suggestion to yourself conforming something that you believe, a set of tasks you'd like to get done that day, a few things you're grateful for, and a few things you're looking forward to.

These things are chosen to help cultivate happiness in the user, as research has shown that focusing on these things throughout your day can make a difference in how you perceive the world around you. Check back into your daily entries to mark tasks as complete or update on how your day went, as well as to mark down anything you think you did well or need to improve on. Above all, have fun with it -- it's your Plannr after all.
